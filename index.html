The great and glorious Giant Panda!

Some random facts:

Pandas eat fast, they eat a lot, and they spend about 12 hours a day doing it. They digest only about a fifth of what they eat.

To get the bamboo to their mouths, they hold the stems with their front paws, which have enlarged wrist bones that act as thumbs for gripping.

Sometimes male pandas relax by doing handstands against trees.

Giant pandas are only about the size of a stick of butter at birth, and they're hairless and helpless.
